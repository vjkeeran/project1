Our Swimming Programs

We offer a variety of classes tailored to different age groups and skill levels, ensuring every child finds the perfect fit to thrive in the water.

Enroll today to secure your spot!

Choose Your Level

Beginner Bubblers (Ages 3-5)

Introduction to water, buoyancy, basic kicks, and breath control. Focus on comfort and fun!

  • Duration: 30 min per session
  • Ratio: 1:4 instructor to student
Intermediate Strokers (Ages 6-9)

Develop freestyle and backstroke. Learn proper breathing techniques and water treading.

  • Duration: 45 min per session
  • Ratio: 1:6 instructor to student
Advanced Divers (Ages 10-14)

Master all four competitive strokes (freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly) and diving.

  • Duration: 60 min per session
  • Ratio: 1:8 instructor to student
Private Lessons (All Ages)

Personalized one-on-one instruction for rapid progression or specific skill focus.

  • Duration: Flexible
  • Ratio: 1:1 instructor to student
Adult Learn-to-Swim (Ages 15+)

It's never too late to learn! Conquer fear of water and develop foundational swimming skills.

  • Duration: 60 min per session
  • Ratio: 1:6 instructor to student
